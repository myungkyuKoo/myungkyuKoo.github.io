About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at KAIST, advised by Jinwoo Shin. I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering at KAIST in 2024. I am broadly interested in a range of problems in computer vision and robotics foundation models. My recent research focuses on Vision-Language-Action models (VLAs), to achieve generalizable, dexterous robot manipulation.
